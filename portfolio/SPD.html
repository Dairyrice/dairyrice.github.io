Photoshop Edit: Nostalgia

  This is an image that I made showcasing a TV show that I grew up watching called Power Rangers. Specifically, the one Power Rangers season called S.P.D.
  One day, my friends and I wanted to look back at all the moments we remembered doing as kids. We wanted to remember the the times when life was just simple and enjoyed having fun.
  And so remembering my time watching a lot of Power Rangers as a kid, I decided to make a piece to have the feeling of nostalgia while being minimalistic at the same time.
  I made this piece using Photoshop by selecting various images and incorporating them into one big image.
