Artist Statement

For this assignment, I approached it knowing that it requires to have pieces connect with each. Whether it would connect to make a system or just a general piece.
When looking at many examples, I assumed that this assignment is making many pieces to create something out of the ordinary. Or create a concept with many pieces.
So with that in mind, I assumed to make a piece that has one main mechanic in fastening while showing at the same time. This idea goes out to most of us in my group.
We wanted to have the idea of having one main mechanism to show many details as much as possible. That is where the idea of a city scape came from. To show the details
that we wanted to show will be city scape builder. The main mechanic of this city builder is make a city based on what you have and make your city how you want it to be.

The things that went well with this assignment was the idea itself and how our pieces were made to fit our theme. We mainly wanted to focus that idea of having a canvas
and build your city how you like it. The things I wish we as a group could've done better was the execution and planning on gettign the pieces we need. One of our problems in
making this piece was the execution in making our pieces, since a lot was missing. In addition, we were still figuring out on what is considered on what type of design it is.
