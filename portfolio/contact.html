Who am I?

My name is Derek Bui. I'm currently a fourth year at San Jose State University and I'm majoring in Digital Media Art.
I'm one of those people that get bored really easily and just works as free time. I always want to have fun and be optimistic in most situations. Throughout my entire life, there have been times in which I needed to do something or else I won't proceed at all. A lot of what I do is mainly make a lot of photo edits and make videos on my free time. I wanted to follow a path in which leads me to show my creative perspective and apply it to my daily life. That is when I found about SJSU's DMA program. Ever since I applied for this major, I learned how to make art in many mediums. It has been a wonderful experience to be apart of. "
