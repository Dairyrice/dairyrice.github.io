Who am I?

	     My name is Derek Bui. I'm currently a fourth year at San Jose State University. I started to major in Digital Media Art when I was trying to figure out what I wanted to major in
       for my college life. After graduating high school, I was still wondering what to do in my life. I was debating with myself to either just go to college and find my future that way,
       or just work for the rest of my life. I was even questioning myself if I was truly happy with the decisions that I made to be satisfied in life. But eventually, I decided to major
       in Digital Media Art because not only will I learn many different skills from many mediums, I feel like taking this major would help me in the future. I wanted to learn the ways of
       making art in the digital spectrum. Throughout my time being in the Digital Media Arts program, I learned many different skills such as photoshop, illustrator, video editing, web
       development, and many more. But the digital mediums that I feel the most comfortable with are mainly video and photography. One of the objectives that I want to achieve for my career
       is to make art for a living and make a business out of it. I always wanted to make a business in making art and make a living out of it. But that is not my priority in terms of my goals
